7 June 2025

Woodrow High House Fun Day

Pippa Kirkbride

High Sheriff of Buckinghamshire

With huge thanks to Woodrow High House for helping me host a High Sheriff Fun Day on Saturday for 50 local young people who would not normally have access to this type of activity.

It was wonderful to see the groups of young people trying the high ropes for the first time, followed by archery and then working as teams helping each other over obstacles and walls.

The level of chat and noise at lunch was infectious; the smiles and laughter said it all. It was a day full of joy.

Special Thanks

Thank you to all my guests who came to support with special note to the Lord-Lieutenant, HJH Jonathan Cooper, Sarah Green MP, Cllr Sarfarez Khan Raja Chairman of Buckinghamshire Council, Cllr Majid Hussain Mayor of High Wycombe and Cllr James Brown Deputy Mayor of Amersham.

The professionalism of the Woodrow High House team was evident wherever you turned and I can't thank you enough for making the day everything I wanted.
