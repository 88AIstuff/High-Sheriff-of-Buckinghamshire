Caldecotte Experience

6 May 2025 High Sheriff of Buckinghamshire

On a beautiful spring evening last Thursday, I was delighted to attend the Grand Opening of the newly refurbished, fully accessible Action 4 Youth building at the Caldecotte Xperience.

The newly refurbished building showcases Action 4 Youth's commitment to creating inclusive spaces for young people across Buckinghamshire. With state-of-the-art facilities and full accessibility features, the Caldecotte Xperience is set to become a vital resource for youth development in our community.

During the event, I had the opportunity to tour the facilities and learn about the various programs that will be offered to young people, including outdoor activities, leadership training, and skill development workshops.

I look forward to returning in the summer and seeing the building in action. Thank you @Jenifer Cameron for your welcome and hospitality.

This initiative aligns perfectly with my vision as High Sheriff to support the young people of Buckinghamshire and foster a lifelong love of volunteering within our community.
